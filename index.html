Introduction

gatecon is a Web3 social gaming platform that aims to penetrate Web2 players to adopt Web3 games. gatecon makes Web3 games accessible and fun. The app will provide a marketplace, rewarding system, shorts, easy payment system, and wallet handling. Users won't be hassled with a crypto wallet, because the app will have the user's familiar login habits: mail, Google account, and crypto wallet. Create and handle user's wallet, transactions, and gains. The app will also have a learning section, which will describe the benefits of Web3 to end users.

Home

Feed

Gamers will be able to share posts, like and comment on posts, send them to each other with the people in their network and interact with the post shares. They will be able to share NFT and their tokens with other players.

Community

Thanks to the communities created for the games, the players will always stay in touch and be dynamic. In addition, game companies will be able to appeal to ready-made communities on the platform instead of creating a community from scratch.

Discover

A space to discover games, users, videos, items. Thanks to the artificial intelligence supported algorithm we have developed, you will get the closest results in your searches. You will be able to automatically link your .ens, .bnb, .cosmos etc. domains.

Gaming

Launchpad

Marketplace

The gatecon marketplace allows users to buy, sell, and trade NFTs and tokens. Users can also use the marketplace to find games that they are interested in playing.

Gamification

Ranking

Videos

The gatecon shorts feature allows users to watch short videos about web3 games. The shorts are a great way for users to learn about new games and get tips on how to play them

Rewarding System

The gatecon rewarding system rewards users for playing games and completing tasks. Users can earn rewards in the form of NFTs, tokens, and other prizes.

Easy Payment System

The gatecon easy payment system makes it easy for users to buy and sell NFTs and tokens. Users can use a variety of payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers.

Wallet Handling

The gatecon wallet handling feature makes it easy for users to manage their crypto wallets. The app will create and handle user's wallet, transactions, and gains. Users will never have to worry about losing their crypto again.

Learning Section

The gatecon learning section provides users with information about web3. The section includes articles, videos, and tutorials that explain the benefits of web3 and how to use it.

Login Mechanisms

gatecon has three login mechanisms: mail, Google account, and crypto wallet. Users can choose the login mechanism that is most convenient for them.

Burning Mechanism

The gatecon burning mechanism allows users to gain cases by playing or winning games. Users can then choose to open the case or sell it. If the user opens the case, they may gain nothing, in which case the burning mechanism will work. If the user opens the case, they may gain tokens or in-game items.

Battlepass System

The gatecon battlepass system allows users to earn rewards by completing challenges. The rewards can include NFTs, tokens, and other prizes.

Leveling System

The gatecon leveling system allows users to earn rewards by leveling up. The rewards can include NFTs, tokens, and other prizes.
